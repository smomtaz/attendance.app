üîÑ How It Works

Simple workflow for students and teachers

1

Student Registration

Teachers register students with their names, IDs, email/phone numbers in a secure database. Each student gets a unique identifier.

2

Location Verification

When students arrive at class, the app verifies their location using GPS geofencing to ensure they're in the classroom.

3

Check-In Process

Students enter their registered email or phone number. The system verifies registration and records attendance for the current session.

4

View Progress

Students access their personal portal with a PIN to view attendance history, quiz scores, and attendance percentage.
